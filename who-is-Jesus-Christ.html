WHO IS JESUS CHRIST?

1. God (John 1:1,14,17; 20:28; Titus 2:13; 1 John 5:20)

2. The Beginning (Revelation 1:8; Colossians 1:18)

3. The Ending (Revelation 1:8)

4. The Maker of all things (John 1:3)

5. The One by Whom all things were created (Col. 1:16; Eph 3:9)

6. The One for Whom all things were created (Col. 1:16; Rev. 4:11)

7. The One Who is before all things (Col.1:17)

8. The One by Whom all things consist (Col. 1:17)

9. Lord and God (John 20:28)

10. One with God the Father (John 10:30)

11. The Express Image of God’s Person (Heb. 1:3)

12. The Brightness of God’s Glory (Heb 1:3)

13. One with God the Father and God the Holy Ghost (1 John 5:7)

14. The One Who upholds all things with the Word of His Power (Heb 1:3)

15. The Almighty (Rev 1:8)

16. The One Who has all power in heaven and in earth (Matthew 28:18)

17. The One with power over all of the devil’s (Luke 10:17-19)

18. The One in Whom is life (John 1:4)

19. The Way, the Truth, the Life (John 14:6)

20. The Resurrection and the Life (John 11:25)

21. Eternal Life (John 17:3)

22. Our Life (Colossians 3:4)

23. The Owner of the kingdoms of the world (Rev 11:5)

24. The Owner of the everlasting kingdom (2 Peter 1:11)

25. The Owner of all that God the Father owns (John 16:15)

26. The Only begotten of God the Father (John 1:14)

27. The Son of God (John 9:35-36; 10:36)

28. The Lamb of God Who takes away the world’s sin (John 1:29)

29. The One born to save His people from their sins (Matthew 1:21)

30. The One manifested to take away our sins (1 John 3:5)

31. The One Who washed us from our sins in His Own Blood (Rev 1:5)

32. The One Whose Blood cleanses from sin (1 John 1:7)

33. The One Who redeemed us by His Blood (1 Peter 1:18-19)

34. The One Whose Blood and Word overcomes the devil (Rev 12:7-11)

35. The One Who enables us to escape the world’s pollution (2 Peter 2:20)

36. The One Who was offered to bear the sins of many (Heb 9:28)

37. The One Whose Body, offered for sins, perfects, sanctifies, forever (Heb10:10-14)

38. The One Who suffered to bring us to God (1 Peter 3:18)

39. The Only Mediator between Sinless God and sinful man (1 Timothy 2:5)

40. The One Who died for our sins (1 Corinthians 15:1-3)

41. The One Who bore our sins in His Body on the cross (1 Peter 2:21-24)

42. The One Who rose from the dead (1 Corinthians 15:4)

43. The First Begotten from the dead (Rev 1:5)

44. He that lives, was dead, is alive evermore (Rev 1:18)

45. He that has the keys of Hell and death (Rev 1:18)

46. The One Who makes us born-again by His Resurrection (1 Peter 1:3)

47. The One Who makes us acceptable to God (1 Peter 2:5)

48. The Apostle and High Priest of our profession of faith (Heb 3:1)

49. The Great High Priest (Heb 4:14)

50. The Advocate (1 John 2:1)

51. The One Who has, and offers, salvation (2 Tim 2:10)

52. The One Who is the Author of Eternal salvation to those who choose to obey Him (Heb 5:9)

53. The One Who has, and offers, grace (2 Tim 2:1; 1 Thess 5:18; 2 Cor 13:14)

54. The One Whose grace is sufficient (2 Cor 12:9)

55. The One Who is able to keep us from falling (Jude 24-25)

56. The One Who delivered us from the wrath to come (1 Thess 1:10)

57. The One Who abolished death (2 Tim 1:10)

58. The One Who brought life and immortality to light through the Gospel (2 Tim 1:10)

59. The One in Whom are all spiritual blessings in heavenly places (Ephesians 1:3)
