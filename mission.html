Article Title with Solid Background

Mission:

“WE’RE GOING INTO ALL THE WORLD,
PREACHING THE GOSPEL
TO EVERY CREATURE”
(Mark 16:15)”

&

“TAKING THE MESSAGE
OF LIVING AS A WITNESS UNTO CHRIST
TO THIS GENERATION”
(Acts 1:8)

According to the Will of our Heavenly Father, in the Name of our Lord, Savior, and Soon-Coming-King Jesus Christ, in the Power of the Holy Spirit, according to the Word of God, the Biblical Mission of Soulwinners Ministries International, is to “GO INTO ALL THE WORLD AND PREACH THE GOSPEL TO EVERY CREATURE!” and “TAKE THE MESSAGE OF LIVING AS A WITNESS UNTO CHRIST TO THIS GENERATION” through:

  1. Christ - Our reaching upward to Christ, in “WE’RE TAKING THIS LIFE FOR JESUS!!!” daily devotional meetings and daily living with our Lord, through Biblical fasting, prayer, reading and studying of God’s Word, loving sin-free Biblical obedience, and faith.
  2. Campus - Christ reaching outward, through us, to students, staff, faculty, and visitors, on college campuses, by our preaching open air “WE’RE TAKING THIS CAMPUS FOR JESUS!!!” meetings to hundreds that gather in the free speech areas, witnessing the Gospel one on one and to smaller groups, preaching and teaching the Word of God in classrooms at the invitations of professors, and exhorting campus ministries to Biblical holiness and the evangelization of their fellows students.
  3. Church - Christ reaching outward, through us, to His Body, the Church, by our preaching and teaching His Word in WE’RE TAKING THIS CHURCH FOR JESUS!!!” meetings of Christians in church buildings, in homes; by our training Christians in Biblical evangelism, by our inviting Christians to accompany us as we preach the Gospel in the open air to crowds and individuals; by our praying, fellowshipping, continuing in the apostles’ doctrine, and breaking bread, with, Christians, in church buildings, in home fellowships, and on an individual basis; by our Biblically interceding with, and for, churches, while both physically preaching and fellowshipping with them, and while physically absent from them; and by our planting, serving, and overseeing, churches of Biblical Christians, in cities, worldwide.
  4. City - Christ reaching outward, through us, to cities, by our preaching and teaching His Gospel and being used by the Holy Spirit to bring salvation from sin, sickness, and satan, to city inhabitants, in city-wide and regional “WE’RE TAKING THIS CITY FOR JESUS” Gospel crusades; training the apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers of cities and regions, in Biblical ministry, in city-wide “CHRIST’S BIBLICAL VISION AND MISSION FOR HIS CHURCH” seminars; training Christians in cities and surrounding regions to live as Biblical witnesses of Christ and preach the Gospel to every creature in “COUNT TO FIVE: EVANGELIZE” Biblical evangelism training seminars; and by our fasting and praying in intercession for cities, in city-wide and regional “WE’RE TAKING THIS CITY FOR JESUS” fasting and intercessory prayer gatherings.
  5. Country - Christ reaching outward, through us, to countries, by our preaching and teaching his Gospel, in WE’RE TAKING THIS COUNTRY FOR JESUS!!!” crusades, seminars, church gatherings, campus meetings, in various prayerfully and strategically selected cities and villages, throughout the country; by our training ministers in seminars throughout the country; by our training Christians in Biblical evangelism throughout the country; by our preaching and teaching the Gospel in the open air to multitudes and individuals on college campuses and in public areas throughout the country; and by our daily fasting and praying and interceding for countries in fasting and intercessory prayer gatherings throughout the country.
  6. Continent - Christ reaching outward, through us, to continents, in “WE’RE TAKING THIS CONTINENT FOR JESUS!!!” meetings/conferences/conventions/holy convocations, held in prayerfully and strategically selected major cities/states/countries/nations, in which hundreds and thousands of Christians with a single eye to “TAKE THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!” attend, then return to their place of residence to TAKE IT FOR JESUS. These “WE”RE TAKING THIS CONTINENT FOR JESUS!!!” will include: “CHRIST’S BIBLICAL VISION AND MISSION FOR HIS CHURCH” seminars to train apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers, in Biblical “WE’RE TAKING THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!” Ephesians 4:7-16 ministry, “COUNT TO FIVE: EVANGELIZE” seminars to train all Christians in Biblical “WE’RE TAKING THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!” Mark 16:15-20 witnessing and evangelism, and “GETHSEMANE GATHERINGS” where those with our Lord’s Brokenhearted burden for the lost and the church can come together in Biblical fasting and intercessory prayer, for a Biblical outpouring of the Holy Spirit, worldwide. Additionally, the “WE’RE TAKING THIS CONTINENT FOR JESUS!!!” convocations will train those in attendance how to prayerfully train those in their families, neighborhoods, churches, ministries, work places, cities, countries, nations, continent, how to both individually and corporately “TAKE THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!”
  7. Communications - Christ reaching outward, through us, to the world through, in not only Gospel preaching worldwide, and training the church worldwide to engage in Gospel preaching worldwide, but also through preaching the Gospel and training others to preach the Gospel through “WE’RE TAKING THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!” communication systems, such as the book that the Lord had us publish on Gospel preaching in 2007, “Journal of a Street Preacher”, the website soulwinner@soul-winners.org which offers free Biblical materials to the world, the television show “Soulwinners TV” which broadcasts the Holy Spirit’s Gospel preaching through us throughout the geographical area of our ministry headquarters in East Lansing, Michigan, USA, the “Count to Five” Evangelize” Biblical evangelism training DVD for Christians, the “Christ’s Biblical Vision and Mission for His Church” Biblical ministry training DVD for apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers, and the “East Africa for Jesus” Biblical Gospel preaching DVDs for both Christians and the lost, the regular email newsletter “Soulwinners Ministries International Update” that we email to recipients around the world, and the “WE’RE TAKING THIS WORLD FOR JESUS INTERNATIONAL PRAYER AND PREACHING MOVEMENT” through which we spiritually partner with preachers and intercessors and believers around the world to TAKE THIS WORLD FOR JESUS through Biblical fasting, prayer, and preaching.
