Vision:

"WE'RE TAKING THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!"
(Acts 17:6)

According to the Will of our Heavenly Father, in the Name of our Lord, Savior, and Soon-Coming-King Jesus Christ, in the Power of the Holy Spirit, according to the Word of God, the Biblical Vision of Soulwinners Ministries International, is to "TAKE THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!" through:
  1. LIVING - Our Biblically living, by the Power of the Holy Spirit, in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ, in Biblical, loving, sin-free, obedient faith, to our Heavenly Father, Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, and the Holy Spirit, and living in Biblical, loving, sin-free, self-denying, cheerful, service to our spouses, childre, siblings, parents, relatives, co-workers, neighbors, strangers, both in the Church and in the world!
  2. PREACHING - Our Biblically preaching, by the Power of the Holy Spirit, in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ to every creature, worldwide, in Africa, Antarctica, Asia, Australia, Europe, North America, and South America.
  3. TEACHING - Our Biblically teaching, by the Power of the Holy Spirit, in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ, Christians to live as witnesses unto Christ, preach the Gospel to every creature, and teach other Christians to do the same. 
  4. TRAINING - Our Biblically training, by the Power of the Holy Spirit, in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ, apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers, in Biblical living and Biblical ministry, to train the Church and the world.
  5. PLANTING/OVERSEEING - Our Biblically planting and/or overseeing, by the Power of the Holy Spirit, in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ, Biblical evangelism training Churches and ministries of Biblically-defined Christians, worldwide, to, in the Name of Jesus, preach the Gospel to every creature, heal the sick, cast out devils, raise the dead, destroy the kingdom of satan, and build the kingdom of God.
